How to use:

YOLO Checker

A quality assurance tool for reviewing datasets. Compares "Clean" vs "Annotated" side-by-side.

How to use:

  • Drag & Drop Multiple Images & CSVs.
  • The tool auto-pairs files by name.
  • Review the side-by-side comparison.
